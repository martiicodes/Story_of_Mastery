Why Transformation Happens
at the Level of Your Thoughts


The Cruel and Everlasting Power of Thoughts



Published on July 09, 2024 by Nenad Milcanovic


Albert Einstein: "The measure of intelligence is the ability to change."

You, like me, have probably heard this mantra many times in your life—that the way you think influences your reality.

In this article, we'll explore how our thoughts serve as a lever for any kind of personal transformation.

If you keep reading, you'll discover the treasure hidden from you. It's hidden in plain sight. You'll understand how to use that lever and experience the best feeling... the feeling of positive change! And it all begins with...

The Devastatingly Powerful Story of Perspectives
"Positive" or "Negative," Your Choice!


Now, imagine all the people in your life who told you: "No, you can't do that!"
Those doubters are the norm. You can't escape this. Embrace it.

You might ask: "How do I combat this?" Doubt and fear are not pleasant emotions, right?
I won’t argue this, but I have a question for you:
Where do doubt, fear, and insecurity come from?

I think the best way to understand this is in terms of perspectives. They are the layers of reality. Everybody has them. And thank God we have them. The problem is if you're not conscious of them.

Being conscious of the layers of reality means: You know that reality is subjective.

That's it. You have a perspective on reality, and so do I. Your parents, friends, kids and partner have different views of life. And that's okay.

If you conceptually zoom out a bit, you'll see that our sub-headlines in this article are also just that — perspectives!

So, the good news is: you have the power to use them and change them for your own purposes. To align your goals in money, career, and love with your map of reality.


In the next section, you'll acquire a simple, yet incredibly powerful map for your success journey. In business or life in general. So, keep reading because we will learn about...

The Vicious Circle That Runs The World
The Tool That Built and Destroyed Empires

Humans have thoughts, emotions, and actions. They're interconnected. They form an unbreakable, ruthless circle. That circle reflects ALL of our lives.

The vicious cycle: Thought - Emotion - Action




Every improvement happens at the level of thought and action. Your actions influence your thoughts as well. That’s why our circle is called "vicious."

Albert Ellis, a psychologist who developed Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT), a precursor to Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), emphasized the interplay between thoughts (mind), emotions, and behaviors: "People are not disturbed by things, but by the views they take of them."

This concept is echoed in modern psychology and neuroscience. Our thoughts can trigger emotions, which then drive our actions, reinforcing the initial thought. This feedback loop can either propel us toward success or lead us into destructive patterns. How to break the cycle?

Understanding this cycle is the first step toward gaining control over it. By challenging and changing our thoughts, we can alter our emotional responses and, consequently, our behaviors.

This is the foundation of many therapeutic approaches, including CBT, which has been proven effective in treating various mental health disorders.

Serena Williams: "I always believe I can win. I have that in my mind. And once you have that in your mind, it’s like a habit."

Our final stop on this journey will bring order and understanding to your everyday strivings toward your goals. So, enjoy this phenomenal concept and learn about...

The Exclusive Secret Club Hidding in Plain Sight
The Intricate Inner Game
of Money, Networking, and Power

You wonder... "Nenad, where's this magical place? This wonderful secret club you are referring to." Well, I don't want to disappoint you, but it's wonderfully simple. It's inside you.

You play a game inside yourself. In reality, inner and outer games are indistinguishable. You are a star athlete ni your domain — think of yourself as Michael Phelps preparing to win gold medals.

Also, you are your own coach. You're talking to yourself inside your head. Understand that nobody can replace your inner coach. She or he knows you better than anyone else. So, listen to your inner coach—your inner guide on your success journey.




How to unlock your potential?

To truly harness the power of the inner game, embrace the following:

Self-awareness: Understand your strengths, weaknesses, and motivations. Self-awareness is the first step toward harnessing the limitless opportunities your inner game brings.

Mastery of Your Mindset: Cultivate a growth mindset. Believe in your ability to develop skills and overcome obstacles. Your thoughts shape your reality.

Resilience: Embrace challenges as opportunities for growth. Be a champion and develop the mental toughness to bounce back from setbacks.

Visualization: Use the power of visualization to see yourself achieving your goals. This mental rehearsal prepares you for success and reinforces your commitment.

Novak Djokovic: “Visualization is something that I do on a daily basis. It’s a part of my life. It helps me to create a picture of what I want to achieve and how I want to play on the court.”

Consistency: Consistent effort, driven by your inner coach's guidance, is key to achieving long-term success. Commit to daily practices that align with your goals.

Inner Dialogue: Maintain a positive and constructive inner dialogue. Your words and thoughts can either fuel your progress or hinder it.

Now, let’s bind everything together. The conclusion section below will bring more light on...




How Mastering Your Thought Process Can Bring You Lasting Transformation

As we journey through the intricate dynamics of personal transformation, remember:

Perspectives Shape Reality: Your perspective on reality is subjective, and understanding this gives you the power to change it. By consciously choosing and adjusting your perspectives, you align your reality with your goals.

The Vicious Cycle: Recognize the cycle of thoughts, emotions, and actions. By altering your thoughts, you can influence your emotions and behaviors, breaking free from destructive patterns and paving the way for success.

Inner Game Mastery: Your inner game is an unparalleled resource.

By nurturing self-awareness, cultivating a growth mindset, embracing resilience, using visualization, maintaining consistency, and fostering positive inner dialogue, you unlock your full potential.
