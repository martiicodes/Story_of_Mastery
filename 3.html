Only Idea You’ll Ever Need
to Win BIG

WARNING! If you want huge success, don't miss these 2 simple steps:

Published on August 08, 2024 by Nenad Milcanovic

1. Define your goals
2. Enjoy the process

You'll get a bonus step at the finish line! It's the master key to success. It unites step 1 and step 2. This step will surprise you!

1. Define Your Goals... and Win like an Elon Musk

Do you feel like a failure? Living meaningless life?

We can't live without goals. Every action is a result of goals, conscious or unconscious. You can't get what you want if you don't aim at it.

Without goals, we're lost and anxious, overwhelmed with an infinite number of possibilities. Without an attractive future to look forward to, life is a nightmare. Without goals, people lose a big source of happiness.

We feel good when we progress toward goals — our own goals, things we truly want.
Goals give us structure. Structure brings order. With order, we feel peace.
Clarify your goals. Your target should be crystal clear in your mind.

Do you want to own the world or wander around, lost and unfulfilled?

2. Enjoy the Process... and Don't Be Ashamed of Your Level of Success

Are you stressed out? Do you feel trapped and miserable? Are you experiencing burnout?

Danger!You forgot why you started in the first place. You're hyper-focused on the results, so you forgot to play. All joy is gone. Without joy, you crumble.

Train yourself to enjoy the process. Every minute, you can be better at what you do.
Progress and improve, but don't forget to enjoy the game.
You're truly happy when you play. This is the flow state.

Bonus Step

Now, how do you unite step 1 and step 2 for maximum success?

Exclusively goal-oriented people are miserable. They're never happy, always hungry, and unable to enjoy the fruits of their hard work.

Exclusively process-oriented people are lost in the maze of infinite possibilities. Life without structure and order.
They try to live in the present moment to cancel suffering. Suffering comes partly from our future.
The future is a burden. Goals live in the future.

You need step 1 and step 2: to define your goal AND to enjoy the process, at the same time.
This may sound paradoxical to you.

Goals define the Game. The Game is a framework for life. It has rules, players, obstructions, and challenges.
Without goals, we have no reason to wake up in the morning.

Enjoying the process emerges when we enter the Game — when the goal is defined.
I guess you want to reach your goal fast. The best way to do that is to enjoy the process.

Step 1 and step 2 feed each other. Together, they form a powerful success loop.

This bonus step unites step 1 and step 2. Be focused on your goals and enjoy the process at the same time.
The bonus step requires you to accept ambiguity. Be obsessed with your goals and results.
At the same time, enjoy the process and don't worry about the results.
